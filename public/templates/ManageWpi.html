You need to create at least one WPI to begin.

Your WPI's list is saved on your computer using HTML5 local storage. It is meant to be a time saving feature, so that you don't need to re-enter it on each session, while at the same time allowing you to switch between different configurations. If you clear the local storage in your browser, your choices here will be lost.

Changes are saved automatically as you edit.

Project, Workspace and Iteration data is cached on your local machine to improve performance. Click the Refresh Rally Data button if you know that they have been updated on the server.
